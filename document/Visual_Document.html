Consistent Design

Design templates based on custom visual component. Standardize visual design for All pages and elements. Use consistent components (tab, form, pagination, table and button etc.). Use consistent margin and padding across all blocks and contents. Design style for channels and columns should match general look and feel throughout the whole site .
